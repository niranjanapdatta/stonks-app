Result

  • An app that performs analysis on the close price of stocks and provides them to the user in form of tables and charts.

  • Comparision of stocks selected by the user with visualization and tables.

  • Insights and Trivia related to the stock market (NSE) which will enlighten the user and aid them in making well thought out decisions.

  • A system that enables one (admin- stock market expert) to add new stocks, insights, trivia and also update or delete them. A user management system is also included.

  • Get the analysis of stock, information about the stock, insights related to the stock- all at one place.
